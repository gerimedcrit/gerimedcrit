About Gerimedcrit

Gerimedcrit provides evidence-based medication timelines for geriatric critical care and autonomic disorders.

Our Mission

To track and visualize clinical trial evidence for medications used in treating orthostatic hypotension and related autonomic conditions, making research accessible to clinicians and researchers.

Methodology

Each medication timeline includes:

  • Randomized controlled trials (RCTs)
  • Systematic reviews and meta-analyses
  • Safety studies and adverse event data
  • FDA approval milestones
  • Published clinical guidelines
